Strawberry Oatmeal Breakfast Smoothie

Home | To Die For Blueberry Muffins | Black Bean Breakfast Bowl

Description

This delicious and vegan-friendly smoothie is a great way to start your day. Creamy and fruity but also filling, you'll want to make this recipe your morning go-to.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 14 frozen strawberries
  • 1 banana, broken into chunks
  • 1 1/2 tsp white sugar (Optional)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Blend soy milk, oats, strawberries, and banana in a blender until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla and blend again until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
