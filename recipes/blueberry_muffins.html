To Die For Blueberry Muffins

Home

Description

Delicious and decadent Blueberry Muffins, the perfect breakfast for any morning. These muffins are quick to make, and sure to impress. The secret: the spiced streusel that will make your muffins the best in town.

Ingredients (yields 8 servings)

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup milk, or more as needed
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Crumb Topping:

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
