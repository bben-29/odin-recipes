Black Bean Breakfast Bowl

Description

Low on carbs and quick to make, this breakfast bowl will fill you up and get you going.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 avocado, peeled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Pour eggs into skillet; cook and stir eggs until set, 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Place back beans in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave at High power until warm, about 1 minute. Divide between two bowls.
  3. Divide scrambed eggs between each bowl; top each with avocado and salsa, then season with salt and black pepper.
